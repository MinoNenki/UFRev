COOKIES POLICY
==============

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Used for functionality, analytics, and security.

2. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

Essential Cookies (Required)
• Session cookies - keep you logged in
• Security cookies - prevent fraud
• Functionality cookies - remember preferences
These cookies are NECESSARY for service operation.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)
• Track page usage
• Measure feature popularity
• Understand user behavior
These help us improve the service.

Marketing Cookies (Optional, if applicable)
• Track conversion
• Measure ad effectiveness
Only if you interact with ads or referral links.

3. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES
Services using cookies:
• Stripe (payments)
• OpenAI API (if logging)
• Vercel Analytics (if enabled)
• Supabase Auth (sessions)
See their privacy policies for details.

4. COOKIE CONSENT
By using our site, you consent to:
✓ Essential cookies (required)
✓ Analytics cookies (can be disabled)
✓ Marketing cookies (can be disabled)

We display a cookie banner on first visit. You can:
• Accept all cookies
• Reject non-essential cookies
• Manage preferences

5. HOW TO CONTROL COOKIES
In your browser:
• Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
• Firefox: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
• Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
• Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Clear cookies anytime - you'll be logged out.

6. COOKIE DURATION
• Session cookies: deleted when you close browser
• Persistent cookies: deleted manually or after 12 months
• Essential cookies: kept for 24 months (security)
